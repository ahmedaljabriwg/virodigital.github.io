Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know.

Do I own my website and domain? +
Yes, fully. Once your build is paid for, the design and content are yours. If we register your domain on your behalf, it is registered in your name with your contact details — we simply manage the technical DNS settings so you don't have to.
I already have a website. Can you improve it? +
Yes. If your current site is slow, outdated, or not showing up correctly on mobile, we can rebuild or redesign it within our standard packages. We'll handle the migration so your existing visitors experience zero downtime.
What if I need to update something on my site? +
It depends on your plan. Viro Start includes a client portal where you can make basic text edits yourself. On Viro Plus and Viro Pro, simply email us — we make the changes for you within your monthly update allowance.
Is there a long-term contract? +
No. All care plans are billed monthly and can be cancelled with 30 days' notice. We don't lock you in — we'd rather earn your business every month.
Why not just use a budget hosting provider? +
Budget hosting gives you a server and nothing else. You're responsible for security, software updates, backups, and anything that breaks. With Viro, you're paying for a fully managed service — we monitor, maintain, and fix everything so you never have to.
I'm not technical. How complicated is this? +
Not complicated at all — that's the point. We handle every technical aspect of your website. Once we have your business details and a few images, we take it from there. We communicate in plain English and make sure you always understand what's happening with your site.
